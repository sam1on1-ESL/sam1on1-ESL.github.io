Introduction

At SAM 1on1, we don't just teach English—we train young minds to express deeply, speak powerfully, and think globally. Our program guides learners from basic sentences to dynamic communication, equipping them to discuss real-world topics and influential people.

Learning Goals

What Your Child Will Learn & Master:

  • Adjective Usage for Expressive Language: Students will learn to use descriptive words to paint vivid pictures and express emotions, opinions, and impressions.
    Example: "The brave soldier faced a terrible storm."
  • Verbs with Conviction: Upgrade from basic verbs (go, eat, like) to stronger, more expressive verbs (explore, admire, achieve).
  • Adverb Accuracy: Master adverbs to add detail and flair to speech.
    Example: "She answered quickly but kindly."
  • Spontaneous Speaking Development: Through Q&A, debates, free-talking games, and roleplay, students build fluency and confidence without a script.

Signature Skill: Discussing Great People

By the end of the program, students will confidently discuss inspiring figures like Nelson Mandela, Helen Keller, and Malala Yousafzai, understanding their lives, using rich vocabulary to describe their traits and impact, and practicing empathy and critical thinking in speech.

Example: "Helen Keller was a courageous woman who proved that determination can overcome any obstacle. She inspired millions with her story."

This is a key selling point for parents who want their children to become global citizens—able to speak with clarity, compassion, and pride.

Bonus Skills

  • Advanced conjunctions (although, however, as a result)
  • Transition phrases (on the other hand, in conclusion)
  • Sentence expansion & paraphrasing
  • Responding to abstract questions like "What does freedom mean to you?"
